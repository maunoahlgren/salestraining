Welcome to the DevOps Consulting Sales Training

This training program is designed to help you transition from selling Atlassian licenses to becoming a successful DevOps consultant. Explore the modules below to get started.

Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC)

Understand the fundamentals of the Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC) and how it forms the foundation for DevOps.

Module 2: Introduction to DevOps

Explore the basics of DevOps and its role in enhancing the SDLC.

Module 3: The DevOps Toolchain

Familiarize yourself with essential tools and processes within a DevOps pipeline.

Module 4: Selling DevOps Consulting Services

Equip the team with strategies for selling DevOps consulting.

Module 5: Mapping Atlassian to DevOps

Leverage existing Atlassian expertise to add value in DevOps discussions.

Module 6: Case Studies and Success Stories

Learn from real-world examples to better understand the impact of DevOps.

Module 7: Advanced Sales Techniques

Enhance your sales techniques specific to DevOps consulting.

Module 8: Continuous Learning and Improvement

Encourage ongoing education and staying updated with industry trends.
